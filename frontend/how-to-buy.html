How to Buy Akita Inu ASA

Akita Inu ASA is an Algorand token. Be careful of tokens with similar names on other blockchains – these are not connected to us. To buy the real Akita Inu ASA, please follow the steps below:

1 Create an Algorand Wallet

Create a wallet that supports ASAs (Algorand tokens). We recommend The Official Algorand Wallet (a mobile app) or My Algo (a website).

2 Connect Your Wallet to Tinyman

Tinyman is the primary DEX for Algorand, and the place where you will soon be swapping Algorand (ALGO) for Akita Inu.

Go to Tinyman and click “Connect to a Wallet” to connect your Algorand wallet.

3 Buy Algorand (ALGO)

You need Algorand (ALGO) to swap for Akita Inu tokens.

The simplest option is buy your ALGO on Tinyman. Visit Tinyman and click the WALLET tab, then “Add Funds via MoonPay”.

The other option, that may work out cheaper, is to buy your ALGO on a crypto exchange like Coinbase or Binance

4 Transfer ALGO to Your Wallet

If you bought your ALGO on Tinyman, MoonPay will send it to your wallet automatically.

If you bought it on a crypto exchange, go there and find the option to transfer or withdraw your ALGO. You can find the address to send it to by opening your Algorand wallet and clicking “Receive”.

5 Opt in to AKITA INU With Your Wallet

From your Algorand wallet, select the option to add a new asset, choosing AKITA INU TOKEN with ASA ID 384303832

Be careful of imitators and look for the verified check mark!

You will need at least 1 ALGO in your wallet to add the new asset.

6 Swap your ALGO for AKITA INU!

From the SWAP tab on Tinyman select Algorand in the first box, and AKITA INU TOKEN (with the verified check mark and ID 384303832) in the second. Swap your chosen amount, and your AKITA INU will arrive in your wallet within seconds!
