How To Buy AKITA INU

   Buy Akita Inu Now  

1 Buy Algorand

You can purchase Algorand from most crypto exchanges like Coinbase or Binance

2 Create an Algorand Wallet

Create a wallet that supports ASAs.

We recommend The Official Algorand Wallet and My Algo

3 Transfer Algorand to Your Wallet

Click on Receive in your Algorand wallet and use the Algo address to send Algorand from your crypto exchange.

4 Opt-in to AKITA INU With Your Wallet

Add a new asset and select AKITA INU or ASA ID # 384303832

Be careful of imitators and look for the verified check mark!

You will need at least one Algo in your wallet to add ASAs*

5 Connect Your Wallet to Tinyman

Tinyman is the primary DEX for Algorand and where you will be purchasing your shiny new AKITA INU TOKENs.

Click on Connect To A Wallet and connect to your Algorand wallet.

6 BUY!

Select Algorand from the first drop-down under the SWAP tab. Search for the AKITA INU TOKEN with the verification check mark and ID # 384303832. You can also trade many other ASAs for AKITA INU!
